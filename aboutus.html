Who we are

Gears & Grains is an artisan bakery that offers a hand-crafted selection of breads, sandwiches and pizzas. We offer a wide selection of local beers and wines. The bakery was originally established in Boulder, CO in 2020 and named after our passion for cycling, bread, and beer. This passion translates to a delicious selection of beer and bread that is easily accessible via nearby bike paths.

Why we do it

When we began envisioning Gears & Grains' creation, we knew that we wanted our passion for baking bread and cycling to be at the center of our creation. We wanted to bring the friendly faces of our community together.
